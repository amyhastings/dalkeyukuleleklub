Become a Member

There are lots of ways to get involved with the Dalkey Ukulele Klub. All you need is a ukulele and a sense of fun! We are always looking for new members, so don't be shy - come along and join in the craic!

Meetings

Come along to our weekly session and meet the gang! We usually get together upstairs at The Queens every Tuesday from 8pm to 10pm.

Email us

Get in touch by emailing Debbie Emerson at emerson.debbie@gmail.com for details on the next meeting and how to join.

Follow us on Facebook

Keep up to date with all our events and news by following us on our Facebook page. You can also message us there for more information!
