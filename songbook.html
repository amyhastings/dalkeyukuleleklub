The Dalkey Ukulele Klub Songbook

The Dalkey Ukulele Klub Songbook is a collection of songs that we play at our weekly meetings. The book contains hundreds of songs, but we are always looking for new songs! If you have a song that you would like to add to the book, please get in touch. In the meantime, the playlist below contains the originals of some of our favourites. We hope you love them as much as we do!
